Who is Tedd!

Born in 1992 in Riat Hills, Kisumu, Kenya. Ted has grown to be handy and tech savy guy. Attended High School where the love for science and technology started. Then headed to uni at JKUAT to pursue Bachelors of Science in Mechatronics Engineereing.

The passion for tech grew further while programming robotic arms as part of the curriculum. In 2019 I set foot in Australia to pursue a Master of Business and Project Management. At this time coding was under the self.

I am a husband, who just fixes random things around the house and cares too much about the precious indoor plants.
